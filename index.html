About Us

Generic Clinic has been proudly serving the community of Generic Town since 1993 and is dedicated to providing the best non-emergency medical care possible. We have a full service staff that are ready to help you with all of your clinical needs. Our highly trained and friendly staff are available 7 days a week. We are located at 1874 Generic St.

Services Provided

Generic Clinic provides services for individuals and families while following all standard medical guidelines and laws. Our clinic provides standard Primary Care Provider services including:

  • Bloodwork/Urinalysis
  • General Health Screenings
  • Allergy Screenings
  • STI Screenings
  • Vaccination and Booster Shots
  • Pregnancy Tests and Prenatal Checkups
  • Annual Checkups
  • DOT/Work Required Medical Checkups
  • Online Appointment Scheduling/Telehealth

We provide these and other non-emergency services in a friendly and safe environment, and we welcome patients of all ages. All insurances are accepted and we accept payment via personal checks or credit/debit cards.

Make an Appointment

Generic Clinic appointments can be made via our phone number or our convenient online scheduler. You are also welcome to come in and scheulde an appointment in person. We apologize, but walk-in appointments are only available for certain screenings. All other appointments must be made prior to arrival.
